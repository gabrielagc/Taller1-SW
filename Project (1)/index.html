Wallows

Wallows is an American alternative rock band based in Los Angeles composed of Dylan Minnette, Braeden Lemasters, and Cole Preston. ... The band released their debut studio album, Nothing Happens (2019), which featured the single "Are You Bored Yet?", followed by their 2020 extended play Remote.
